DMCA Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

MEWO PROJECT respects the intellectual property rights of others. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), we will respond quickly to claims of copyright infringement.

1. Reporting Infringement

If you believe that your work has been copied in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please provide our agent with the following information:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Detailed contact information (email is preferred).
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate.

2. Contact Information

All DMCA notices should be sent to our official support channels or via our Discord server through the administration ticket system. We take these matters seriously and will investigate within 72 hours.

3. Repeat Infringers

It is our policy to terminate the access of users who are found to be repeat infringers of intellectual property rights.
